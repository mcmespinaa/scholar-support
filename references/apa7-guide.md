# APA 7 Quick Reference Guide

## In-Text Citations

| Situation | First Citation | Subsequent Citations |
|-----------|---------------|---------------------|
| 1 author | (Mehmood, 2016) | (Mehmood, 2016) |
| 2 authors | (Moore & Westley, 2011) | (Moore & Westley, 2011) |
| 3+ authors | (Avelino et al., 2019) | (Avelino et al., 2019) |
| Organisation | (European Commission [EC], 2023) | (EC, 2023) |
| Direct quote | (Evans et al., 2017, p. 601) | -- |
| Narrative | Senge et al. (2015) argue that... | -- |
| Multiple sources | (Avelino et al., 2019; Moore & Westley, 2011) | -- |

### Rules
- Use "&" inside parenthetical citations
- Use "and" in narrative citations
- List multiple sources alphabetically separated by semicolons
- Include page numbers for direct quotes
- Use "et al." from first citation for 3+ authors

## Reference List Formats

### Journal Article
Author, A. A., Author, B. B., & Author, C. C. (Year). Title of article. *Journal Name*, *Volume*(Issue), Pages. https://doi.org/xxxxx

### Book
Author, A. A. (Year). *Title of work* (edition). Publisher.

### Book Chapter
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of chapter. In B. B. Editor (Ed.), *Title of book* (pp. xx-xx). Publisher.

### Website
Author, A. A. (Year, Month Day). *Title of page*. Site Name. https://url

### Report
Organisation. (Year). *Title of report* (Report No. xxx). Publisher. https://url

## Formatting Rules
- References listed alphabetically by first author's surname
- Hanging indent: first line flush left, subsequent lines indented 0.5 inches
- Double-space all entries
- Italicise journal names and volume numbers
- Italicise book and report titles
- Include DOI as hyperlink when available
- Use https:// format for URLs (no "Retrieved from")
